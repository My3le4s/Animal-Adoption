At Whiskers & Wags, we carefully select pets from reputable shelters and rescue organizations to ensure they are in need of a loving home. Our team works closely with these partners to understand each pet's history and needs, ensuring that we only bring in pets that are ready for a fresh start with a new family.

Once the pets arrive at our center, they undergo thorough health checks and receive any necessary vaccinations. Our dedicated team provides essential grooming and care, including baths, nail trims, and basic dental cleaning. We aim to make each pet comfortable and ready for adoption, ensuring they are healthy and happy.
